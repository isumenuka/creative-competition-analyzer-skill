# creative-competition-analyzer-skill
A skill to analyze competition guidelines and generate 10 winning-caliber ideas

## Installation
```
npx skills add https://github.com/isumenuka/creative-competition-analyzer-skill --skill creative-competition-analyzer
```

## What It Does

This skill helps you win creative competitions by:

1. **Analyzing the brief** — reads competition guidelines and extracts what judges *actually* reward, not just what the rules say
2. **Evaluating your concept** — if you share a work-in-progress, it maps your idea against real winner patterns and finds the single highest-leverage improvement
3. **Generating 10 winning-caliber ideas** — each idea is calibrated against 16 real competition winner case studies, so every idea leads with an editorial concept, bridges the physical/digital, and solves a specific real-world problem

## Winner Patterns Built In

The skill is trained on 16 real competitions (Bubble, Rive, Spline, Kittl, Flora, Morphic, and more). The 7 winner patterns it uses as a filter:

- Strong editorial concept first
- Digital made tangible / real-world made digital
- Unexpected combinations that feel obvious in hindsight
- Pixel-perfect craft and consistency
- Pushing the tool to its true limits
- Highly relatable problem solved elegantly
- Emotional storytelling with narrative structure

## Trigger Phrases

This skill activates when you:
- Share a competition brief, hackathon spec, or design challenge
- Ask for ideas or "wow factor" angles for a competition
- Mention platforms like Contra, Bubble, Rive, Spline, Kittl, Flora
- Ask "what do judges look for?" or "could my idea win?"
